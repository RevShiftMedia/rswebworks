What We Do

Electrical Services
Done Safe & Right

From small repairs to complete panel upgrades and EV charging installation, Voltline handles every residential electrical job in the Mesa area with licensed, insured technicians.

Panel Upgrades & Replacements

Old 100A panel holding you back? We upgrade to 200A or 400A service to support modern appliances, EV chargers, and home additions — fully permitted and inspected.

Wiring & Rewiring

Knob-and-tube, aluminum wiring, or aging circuits that trip breakers constantly — we rewire safely and to current code, protecting your home and your insurance coverage.

Outlets & Switches

Dead outlets, GFCI issues, adding USB receptacles, or upgrading to smart switches — we handle all outlet and switch work quickly and cleanly.

EV Charging Installation

Tesla, Rivian, or any other EV — we install Level 2 EVSE chargers with the dedicated 240V circuit your vehicle needs for overnight charging at home.

Lighting Installation

Recessed lighting, ceiling fans, outdoor security lights, landscape lighting — Voltline handles design and installation to transform any space in your home.

Emergency Electrical

Sparking outlets, burning smell, tripped main breaker — electrical emergencies can't wait. We dispatch fast to diagnose and secure your home safely.
