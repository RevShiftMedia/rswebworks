What We Do
Electrical services for
homes & businesses

Every job backed by licensed electricians, clean permit pulls, and a workmanship guarantee. No subcontractors, no surprises.

Panel Upgrades

Outdated 100A panel holding you back? We upgrade to 200A or 400A service—ready for EVs, solar, and modern loads. Full permit pulled.

EV Charger Installation

Level 2 home charging station installation for Tesla, Ford, Rivian, and all EVs. We handle the dedicated 240V circuit and mounting—start to finish.

Whole-Home Rewiring

Knob-and-tube, aluminum wiring, or old cloth-wrapped cables? We rewire completely—bringing older homes up to NEC code and modern safety standards.

Lighting & Fixtures

Recessed cans, LED retrofits, ceiling fans, under-cabinet lighting, and landscape lighting. We handle design-to-install so the results actually look good.

Emergency Electrical

Power out, breaker tripping, burning smell, or sparking outlet? We're available 24/7 for same-day emergency response across the Mesa area.

Commercial Electrical

Tenant build-outs, parking lot lighting, three-phase service, data center circuits—we're equipped for commercial and light industrial work of any scale.
