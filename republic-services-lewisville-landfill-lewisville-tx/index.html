What We Handle

Comprehensive Disposal Services

From residential cleanup to large-scale commercial and construction waste — our permitted facility handles it all with environmental responsibility.

Residential Disposal

Household waste, furniture, appliances, and bulky items. Drop off at our facility or schedule a pickup for large volumes.

Commercial Waste

Regular commercial MSW service for businesses, restaurants, and multi-tenant properties throughout Lewisville and DFW.

C&D Debris Hauling

Construction and demolition debris removal — concrete, drywall, lumber, roofing, and mixed C&D accepted at competitive rates.

Yard Waste & Green Waste

Industrial Disposal

Heavy industrial waste streams, non-hazardous manufacturing byproducts, and large-scale cleanup projects handled efficiently.
