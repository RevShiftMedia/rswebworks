What We Do

HVAC Services Built
for Desert Heat

Las Vegas summers are brutal. ArcticAir has the experience, the parts, and the trained technicians to keep your home cool no matter what the thermostat says outside.

AC Repair

Warm air, strange noises, or a system that won't turn on — we diagnose and repair all central air conditioning issues. Most repairs completed same day.

Heating Repair

Vegas winters get cold at night. We repair gas furnaces, heat pumps, and electric heating systems to keep your home comfortable year-round.

New System Installation

From system sizing to final startup, we install high-efficiency AC and heating systems from leading brands — with financing options available.

Duct Cleaning & Sealing

Leaky or dirty ductwork can increase your energy bills by up to 30%. We clean, seal, and re-insulate ducts so your system runs at full efficiency.

AC Tune-Up & Maintenance

Annual tune-ups extend system life and prevent expensive breakdowns. Our 21-point inspection covers everything before the summer heat arrives.

Emergency HVAC Service

AC out in 110° heat? We prioritize true emergencies. Our 24/7 dispatch gets a technician to your door fast — nights, weekends, and holidays.
