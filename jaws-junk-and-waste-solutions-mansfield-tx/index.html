FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to what Mansfield customers ask us most.

How fast can you come out?
We offer same-day and next-day appointments across Mansfield and surrounding areas. In many cases we can arrive within a few hours of your call — just reach out and we'll find a time that works.
How is pricing determined?
Our pricing is based on the volume and type of junk being removed. We provide a firm, upfront quote on-site before any work begins — no hourly rates, no hidden fees, no surprises on your final bill.
What items do you accept?
We haul almost everything: furniture, appliances, electronics, construction debris, yard waste, mattresses, hot tubs, and general household junk. The few exceptions are hazardous materials like paint, chemicals, and asbestos.
Do I need to be home during the removal?
Not necessarily. As long as we can access the junk and you've approved the quote in advance, our crew can complete the job without you present. We'll send a confirmation when the work is done.
Do you donate or recycle?
Yes. We sort items and divert as much as possible from the landfill — donating usable furniture and goods to local Mansfield-area charities and recycling materials whenever feasible.
How do I prepare for the crew's arrival?
Very little prep is needed on your end. Just make sure we have access to the items being removed. You don't need to move anything or break anything down — our crew handles all the heavy lifting and loading from wherever the junk is located.
Are there any items you won't take?
We handle nearly everything, but we cannot transport hazardous waste including motor oil, paint, pesticides, asbestos, or medical waste. For those materials, your local Mansfield city services or a certified hazmat hauler is the right call. Everything else — we've got you covered.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, credit cards, and most major payment apps. Payment is collected after the job is complete and you're satisfied with the work — not before. We keep the process simple and straightforward from start to finish.
