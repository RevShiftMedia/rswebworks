What We Do

San Antonio Plumbing Services

From routine maintenance to emergency repairs, Roland's covers every plumbing need with over four decades of hands-on experience in San Antonio homes and businesses.

Drain Cleaning

Slow drains and stubborn clogs disrupt your whole household. We use professional hydro-jet and snake equipment to clear blockages fast and restore full flow to your sinks, tubs, and sewer lines — with no mess left behind.

Water Heater Service

No hot water is a major inconvenience, especially on a cold San Antonio morning. Roland's installs, repairs, and replaces tank and tankless water heaters — and we carry units in stock so most jobs are done same day.

Pipe Repair & Replacement

Corroded, cracked, or burst pipes can cause serious water damage inside your walls and foundation. Our team diagnoses the problem accurately, gives you an honest upfront quote, and gets the repair done with durable materials built to last.

Leak Detection

A hidden leak can waste thousands of gallons and drive up your water bill before you ever notice the damage. We use non-invasive acoustic and pressure-testing equipment to pinpoint leaks inside slabs, walls, and underground lines precisely.

Sewer Line Inspection

Tree roots and aging clay pipes can silently damage your sewer line for years. We run a camera inspection to see exactly what's happening underground — so you get a clear diagnosis and don't pay for unnecessary digging or guesswork.

Emergency Plumbing

Plumbing emergencies don't wait for business hours — and neither do we. Roland's is available around the clock, 365 days a year, to respond to burst pipes, sewage backups, flooding, and any other urgent plumbing crisis across San Antonio.
