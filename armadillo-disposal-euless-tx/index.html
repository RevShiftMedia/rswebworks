FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to what Euless customers ask us most before booking.

How quickly can you come out?
We offer same-day and next-day appointments across Euless and surrounding areas. In most cases we can arrive within a few hours of your call — reach out and we'll lock in a time that works for you.
How is pricing determined?
Pricing is based on the volume and type of junk being removed. We provide a firm, upfront quote on-site before any work begins. No hourly rates, no hidden fees — the price we quote is the price you pay.
What items do you haul away?
We take almost everything: furniture, appliances, electronics, construction debris, yard waste, mattresses, hot tubs, and general household junk. The only exceptions are hazardous materials like chemicals, paint, and asbestos.
Do I need to be home during the removal?
Not always. As long as we can access the junk and you've approved the quote in advance, we can complete the job without you present. We send a confirmation once the work is done and everything is cleaned up.
Do you donate or recycle items?
Yes — we divert as much as possible from the landfill. Usable furniture and goods are donated to local Euless-area charities, and recyclable materials are sorted and processed responsibly.
How do I prepare for the crew's arrival?
Very little prep is needed on your end. Just make sure we have access to the items being removed. You don't need to move anything or break anything down — our crew handles all the heavy lifting and loading from wherever the junk is located.
Are there any items you won't take?
We handle nearly everything, but we cannot transport hazardous waste including motor oil, paint, pesticides, asbestos, or medical waste. For those materials, your local Euless city services or a certified hazmat hauler is the right call. Everything else — we've got you covered.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, credit cards, and most major payment apps. Payment is collected after the job is complete and you're satisfied with the work — not before. We keep the process simple and straightforward from start to finish.
Can you handle large or heavy items like hot tubs or safes?
Yes — our crews are equipped and trained to handle oversized, heavy items including hot tubs, exercise equipment, large safes, and pianos. Just let us know in advance so we can bring the right team and equipment for the job in Euless.
Do you serve surrounding areas outside Euless?
Absolutely. While Euless is our home base, we regularly serve customers throughout the DFW Metroplex. Check out our service area section above or give us a call — if you're within our range, we'll make it work and get you scheduled quickly.
