What We Do

Full-Service Plumbing Solutions

From slow drains to full sewer line replacements, Foster Plumbing handles it all — fast, clean, and done right.

Drain Cleaning

Slow or completely blocked drains cleared with professional equipment. Kitchen, bath, floor drains — no clog is too stubborn for our team.

Water Heater Repair

No hot water? We diagnose and repair all makes and models, from traditional tank units to tankless systems. Fast turnaround guaranteed.

Pipe Repair

Cracked, corroded, or burst pipes repaired quickly to prevent water damage. We work on copper, PVC, PEX, and galvanized steel lines.

Leak Detection

Hidden leaks damage foundations and spike water bills. We use advanced detection to pinpoint leaks behind walls and underground — no guesswork.

Sewer Line Service

Sewer backups and line damage handled with camera inspection and trenchless repair options. Protect your Tucson home from costly sewer failures.

Emergency Plumbing

Plumbing emergencies hit at the worst times. Call us any hour — our technicians are on-call 24/7 to stop the damage fast and restore your peace of mind.
