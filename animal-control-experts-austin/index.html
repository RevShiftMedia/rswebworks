What We Do

Complete Wildlife & Pest Control Services

Every job handled with care for the animal and lasting results for your home.

Wildlife Removal

Raccoons have torn through your attic insulation and they won't leave on their own. We use live traps and exclusion techniques to remove them safely, then secure every entry point so they never return.

Rodent Control

Mice and rats in your walls are more than a nuisance — they chew wiring and carry disease. Our integrated approach targets active colonies, seals all intrusion points, and eliminates harborage areas for good.

Bat Exclusion

A bat colony in your roof can deposit hundreds of pounds of guano before you know it's there. We install one-way exclusion devices that let bats leave without re-entering, fully compliant with Texas wildlife regulations.

Bird Control

Pigeons and starlings nesting on your commercial or residential property create sanitation hazards and structural damage. We deploy humane deterrents — spikes, netting, and visual repellents — that last for years.

Attic Restoration

Wildlife contamination leaves insulation soaked with urine and riddled with feces. We remove damaged insulation, sanitize with EPA-registered disinfectants, and re-insulate to restore your attic to pre-infestation condition.

Snake Removal

Finding a snake in your garage or yard stops everything — especially in Austin where copperheads and rattlesnakes are common. Our certified handlers safely identify, capture, and relocate snakes far from your property.

The Process

Fast, Humane, and Done Right

Three straightforward steps from first call to permanent solution.

1
Call for Free Assessment

Call or submit a request and we'll schedule a same-day or next-day inspection. Our specialist identifies the species, entry points, and extent of the intrusion — completely free, no obligation.

2
Humane Capture & Removal

We deploy species-appropriate traps or exclusion devices and monitor them until the animals have been safely captured or have vacated. All animals are relocated to suitable habitat well away from your property.

Once all animals are removed, we seal every gap, vent, and breach using professional-grade materials rated to withstand wildlife pressure. We document every repair and provide a written exclusion warranty.
