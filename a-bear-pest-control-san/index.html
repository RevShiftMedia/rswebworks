What We Treat

Complete Pest Control for San Antonio Homes

From German cockroaches to fire ants, our licensed technicians eliminate the pests that disrupt your family's comfort and safety.

Roach Control

Cockroaches hiding in your kitchen cabinets contaminate food and spread bacteria fast. Our targeted gel bait and residual treatments eliminate the infestation at the source and prevent new ones from moving in.

Rodent Removal

Rats and mice in the walls or attic can chew wiring and spread disease throughout your home. We locate every entry point, set professional traps, and seal gaps so rodents cannot return once cleared.

Termite Treatment

Termites silently destroy structural wood for years before visible damage appears. Our liquid Termidor barrier and bait station systems eliminate active colonies and provide long-term protection for your home's value.

Mosquito Control

Mosquitoes are turning your San Antonio backyard into a no-go zone and putting your family at risk for West Nile and Zika. Our recurring barrier spray program dramatically reduces populations so you can enjoy the outdoors again.

Bed Bug Treatment

Waking up with unexplained bites means bed bugs may have already spread to multiple rooms. Our heat treatment and targeted chemical application eliminates every life stage — eggs included — for a permanent solution.

Ant Control

Fire ants in the yard and sugar ants in the kitchen are more than a nuisance — they can sting family members and compromise stored food. Our colony-elimination treatments stop them at the queen, not just the workers you see.
