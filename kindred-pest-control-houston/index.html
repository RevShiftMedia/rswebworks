What We Treat

Comprehensive Protection Against Every Houston Pest

Houston's subtropical heat and humidity create ideal conditions for pests year-round. We've treated every species in the region — and we know exactly how to eliminate them.

Roach Control

German cockroaches breed rapidly in Houston's heat. Our targeted gel baiting and crack-and-crevice treatment collapses colonies fast — with lasting preventative barriers.

Rodent Removal

Roof rats and Norway rats are common in Houston neighborhoods. We seal entry points, set strategic traps, and monitor until your property is completely rodent-free.

Termite Treatment

Subterranean termites cause catastrophic structural damage silently. Our liquid Termidor barrier and Sentricon bait station system defends your foundation year-round.

Mosquito Control

Houston mosquito season runs nearly year-round. Our monthly barrier spray reduces yard populations by up to 90% per treatment, so you can actually use your outdoor space again.

Bed Bug Treatment

Our combined heat and EPA-registered chemical treatment eliminates bed bugs at every life stage. We don't stop until your home is certified clear — and you sleep soundly again.

Ant Control

Fire ants, carpenter ants, and odorous house ants are all common in Houston. We identify the exact species, then deploy targeted colony-elimination strategies that actually last.
