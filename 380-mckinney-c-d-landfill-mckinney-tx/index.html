Disposal Services

Complete C&D Disposal Solutions

From single loads to commercial volume accounts — we handle construction waste at every scale.

Residential Drop-Off

Homeowners can drop off renovation debris, roofing materials, and demolition waste by the load. Quick, affordable, and hassle-free.

Commercial Hauling Accounts

Set up a commercial account for recurring disposal needs. Volume pricing available for contractors, builders, and demolition companies.

Concrete & Masonry Recycling

We accept concrete, brick, block, and masonry debris and process it for recycled aggregate — keeping it out of general landfills.

Clean Fill Acceptance

Dirt, clay, and clean soil accepted for fill purposes. Ideal for excavation contractors who need to offload cleanly certified material.

Dumpster & Roll-Off Unload

Bring your roll-off dumpster loads directly to our facility. Fast unload service with crew assistance available for large loads.

Asphalt & Pavement Disposal

Shingles, tar paper, asphalt pavement, and paving materials accepted. We process and divert materials from landfill where possible.

The Process

Simple Drop-Off in 3 Steps

Disposing of construction waste at 380 McKinney C&D is fast and straightforward.

01

Call or Drive In

Call ahead to confirm your material is accepted and get a quote, or simply drive in during operating hours. No appointment required for most drop-offs.

02

Weigh In at the Scale

Pull onto our certified scales for a pre-disposal weigh-in. Our team will direct you to the correct disposal area for your material type.

03

Unload & You're Done

Unload in the designated zone, weigh out, and pay at the gate. Quick, clean, and done. Billing accounts available for regular haulers.
