Panel Upgrades

EV Charger Installation

Plugging your Tesla into a standard outlet gives you 4 miles of range per hour. We install a dedicated 240V Level 2 circuit and NEMA 14-50 or hardwired charger so you wake up to a full battery — every morning.

Recessed Lighting

Those old popcorn-ceiling fixtures are dated and dark. We run clean circuits, cut precise cans, and install dimmable LED recessed lighting that transforms how your kitchen or living room feels — no drywall chaos.

Ceiling Fan Install

Mounting a fan to an old light box is a code violation waiting to fall on someone's head. We install a rated fan brace box, run the wiring correctly, and have your new fan balanced and spinning safely in one visit.

Whole-House Surge Protection

One Arizona monsoon surge can wipe out your HVAC, appliances, and smart home gear in a millisecond. A panel-mounted surge protector intercepts that spike before it reaches a single outlet — protecting thousands in electronics for a few hundred dollars.

Emergency Electrical

Burning smell from an outlet, half your house lost power, or a breaker that won't reset — electrical emergencies don't wait until Monday. We dispatch fast, diagnose the root cause, and make your home safe before we leave.
