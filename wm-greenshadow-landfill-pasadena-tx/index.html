Our Services

Comprehensive Waste Solutions

From residential curbside to industrial volumes — our Pasadena facility handles it all responsibly.

Residential Collection

Dependable curbside pickup for homes across Pasadena and Southeast Houston. Consistent schedules you can count on.

Commercial Waste Service

Container service for businesses of all sizes. Scheduled pickup, dumpster placement, and billing accounts for volume customers.

Roll-Off Container Rental

10 to 40-yard roll-off dumpsters for construction, renovation, and commercial cleanup projects delivered to your site.

Recycling Programs

Single-stream recycling pickup for residential and commercial customers. We divert recyclables from landfill at scale.

Construction & Demolition

C&D waste disposal for contractors and demolition crews. Permitted acceptance of concrete, lumber, drywall, and mixed debris.

Special & Industrial Waste

Permitted acceptance of special waste streams including industrial byproducts and non-hazardous processed materials.
