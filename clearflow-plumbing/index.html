What We Do

Plumbing Services
Done Right

From routine maintenance to full-scale emergencies, Clearflow has the expertise and equipment to handle every plumbing challenge in San Antonio.

Drain Cleaning

Slow or completely blocked drain? We use professional hydro-jetting and snaking to clear any clog fast — kitchen, bathroom, or main line.

Water Heater Repair & Replacement

No hot water? We repair and replace all brands of water heaters — tank and tankless. Most jobs done same day with parts on the truck.

Pipe Repair & Leak Detection

Hidden leaks cost thousands if ignored. Our electronic detection equipment finds leaks behind walls and under slabs without unnecessary damage.

Sewer Line Services

Camera inspections, sewer line cleaning, and full replacements. We handle root intrusions, collapses, and aging clay or cast iron lines.

Emergency Plumbing

Burst pipe at 2am? We answer the phone. Our emergency crews are dispatched around the clock because plumbing disasters don't keep business hours.

Bathroom & Kitchen Remodel Plumbing

Relocating fixtures, installing new vanities, or rough-in plumbing for a full remodel — Clearflow keeps your renovation on schedule.
